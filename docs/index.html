Live map and market terminal. Prices, news, predictions, and local activity — one screen, real-time.

Capabilities
  • Map — flights, traffic, earthquakes, weather overlays
  • Markets — 167 assets, Kelly sizing, Fibonacci levels, PnL
  • Portfolio — holdings, budgets, debt payoff, spending analysis
  • News — GDELT, Polymarket predictions, live ticker bar
  • Auth — sessions, Stripe billing, Apple Pay
  • PWA — offline service worker, installable
