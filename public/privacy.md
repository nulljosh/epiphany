# Privacy Policy

**Epiphany** — Portfolio Intelligence Platform

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

**Status:** PIPEDA Compliance Draft — Requires legal review before charging for the service.

## 1. Introduction

Epiphany ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Epiphany mobile application and website.

This policy applies to residents of Canada and is designed to comply with the **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)**.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Avatar image (optional)
- Account creation and login history

### 2.2 Financial Data
- Brokerage account holdings (stocks, ETFs, crypto, cash balances)
- Net worth and portfolio composition
- Transaction history (from connected brokerages)
- Budget and spending patterns

### 2.3 Location Data
- Latitude/longitude for the map-based Situation view
- Local event and venue information

### 2.4 Usage Data
- App feature usage (which screens visited, how long spent)
- Device model and OS version
- Crash reports and error logs
- Feature preferences (map layers, chart types, notifications)

### 2.5 Subscription Data
- Subscription tier (Free or Paid)
- Purchase and billing history
- App Store receipt information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve the Epiphany app and services
- Display your portfolio, holdings, and financial data
- Generate portfolio signals and educational analysis
- Process subscription and billing
- Respond to your requests and support inquiries
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse

**We do not:**
- Sell your personal or financial data to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Share brokerage credentials with any service other than SnapTrade

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 Service Providers
We share information with:
- **SnapTrade** (brokerage data aggregator) — your API credentials and brokerage login (encrypted)
- **Apple** (subscription provider) — email and subscription tier
- **Vercel** (hosting provider) — aggregated analytics and error logs
- **Upstash Redis** (data cache) — session tokens and temporary portfolio data

### 4.2 Legal Requests
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Sensitive data (credentials, API keys) encrypted at rest
- Regular security audits and updates
- Limited employee access to personal data
- No storage of brokerage passwords (credentials are encrypted and stored in the OS keychain)

## 6. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction:** Correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt-Out:** Disable certain data collection features

To exercise these rights, email: joshua@heyitsmejosh.com

## 7. Retention

- Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
- After account deletion, personal data is deleted within 30 days (except as required by law)
- Financial data from connected brokerages is cached temporarily and synced daily
- Crash reports and usage logs are retained for 90 days

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

The Epiphany app does not use cookies. The Epiphany website uses essential cookies for:
- Session authentication
- Security (CSRF protection)
- Analytics (Vercel analytics, no third-party trackers)

### 9.2 Apple App Store

- Review Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/privacy

### 9.3 Vercel
- Hosts the web app and API
- Review Vercel's privacy policy: vercel.com/privacy
