Privacy Policy

Epiphany — epiphany.heyitsmejosh.com · Last updated June 11, 2026

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and name, used to create and secure your account.
  • Location: when you grant permission, your approximate location is used to show nearby earthquakes, flights, incidents, weather alerts, and local places on the map. Location is used for these requests only and is not stored as a history.
  • Financial data: portfolio data you enter, and — only if you link a brokerage — holdings and balances retrieved through SnapTrade. Brokerage credentials are never seen or stored by Epiphany; linking happens on the brokerage's own portal.
  • Profile photo: if you set one, it is stored to display your avatar.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Third-party services

Market data, news, and map data come from public data providers. Brokerage connectivity is provided by SnapTrade; payment processing on the web is provided by Stripe. Each processes only the data needed to provide its service.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account (and all associated data) at any time from Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing us.
